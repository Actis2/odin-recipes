Lasagna Recipe

Prep Time: 30 min

Cook Time: 2 hrs 30 min

Additional Time: 15 mins

Servings: 12

    Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
    • crushed
  • 1 can of crushed tomato
  • 2 cans of canned tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1.5 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzerella, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan

    Directions

  1. Gather all your ingredients
  2. Cook beef, onion and garlic in a dutch oven over medium heat until browned
  3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water. Season with sugar, 2 tbsp parsley, basil, 1 tsp salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered for about 1.5 hours, stir occasionally
  4. Bring a large pot of lighlty salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 min. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water.
  5. In mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, 2 tbsp parsley, 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Preheat oven to 375 F
  7. Assemble the lasgna in a 9x13inch dish
  8. Cover in Foil and bake in oven for 25 min. Remove foil and bake another 25 min
  9. Let cool for 15 min
