Eggs and Rice

Ingredients

You will need Eggs

And you will need rice

Also some butter

Directions

  1. Make rice in rice cooker
  2. Heat frying pan on medium to high
  3. Add your favorite oil or butter to pan
  4. Crack eggs and add to pan, careful not to break the yolk
  5. Let sizzle until egg is mostly white on bottom
  6. Optional: Flip the egg for overeasy, or remove the egg for Sunny-Side Up
  7. Optional: Add a touch of salt and pepper, or your favorite tabasco sauce
  8. Place on rice on plate and enjoy!
