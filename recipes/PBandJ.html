Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Prep Time: 5

Servings: 1

    Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

    Directions

  1. Place the bread down on a plate
  2. Using a butterknife, take the desired amount of Jelly from the container and place it on one of the bread slices, distribute it evenly around to the edges and center.
  3. Do the same thing but with the peanut butter this time.
  4. Place the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and Jelly facing each other
  5. Optional: Cut in half in your desired orientation.
  6. Let sit for 0 min before consuming!
